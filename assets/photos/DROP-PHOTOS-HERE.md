# Drop photos here

The cut photos in this folder (`cut1`–`cut6`, `logo`) were pulled from the public **Booksy** gallery.

Facebook / Instagram photos are token-blocked and could not be fetched automatically. If you want to
add newer or higher-resolution shots (from IG/Facebook or your phone):

1. Drop the image files into this folder (`assets/photos/`).
2. Prefer JPEG/WebP, long edge ~1200px, optimized to **under ~400KB** each.
3. Add or swap them in `index.html` (hero `<img>` and the `.gallery` tiles) with descriptive `alt` text.

Current images and where they're used:

- `cut6.jpeg` — hero (curly top + beard blend)
- `cut2.jpeg` — gallery, tall (waves fade + beard)
- `cut5.jpeg` — gallery + About (box braids + beard)
- `cut3.jpeg` — gallery (kid's taper)
