About Kolby

One chair. Full focus. No rushing your cut.

KobetheBarber is a personal brand, not a factory line. Since 2021, Kolby Williams has built a reputation in Gonzales for cuts that come out exactly the way clients picture them — from designs and razor work to patient first cuts for the little ones.

The reviews say it best: precise, professional, and genuinely good with kids. If you want a barber who listens and takes his time, you found him.
